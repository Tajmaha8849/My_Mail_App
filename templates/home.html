How It Works

Step 1: Register

Click on the Register button and create your account by filling in your username, email, and password.

Step 2: Login

Use your registered credentials to log in to your account and access the dashboard.

Step 3: Send Emails

On the dashboard, fill in the email details (sender, recipient, subject, and body) and click Send Email.

Step 4: View Sent Emails

Check the Sent Emails section on the dashboard to view previously sent emails.

Step 5: Logout

Click the Logout button to securely end your session and return to the home page.
